# chat2repo Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 3, 2025
**Last Updated:** December 3, 2025

## Introduction

chat2repo ("we," "our," or "us"), developed by Flout Labs, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our browser extension.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide
- **GitHub Personal Access Token (PAT)**: Stored locally in your browser to authenticate with GitHub
- **Repository Configuration**: Owner, repository name, branch, and file path preferences
- **Default Tags**: Your preferred default tags for notes

### 2. Information Automatically Collected
- **Page URLs**: Only when you explicitly capture content
- **Page Titles**: Only when you explicitly capture content
- **Selected Text/Messages**: Only the content you explicitly choose to save

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Personal information beyond what you configure
- ❌ Background tracking of any kind
- ❌ Analytics or telemetry
- ❌ Cookies or tracking pixels
- ❌ Your GitHub password (only PAT with limited scope)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
- **Save Notes to GitHub**: Create markdown files in your configured repository
- **Authenticate with GitHub**: Use your PAT to access the GitHub API
- **Remember Preferences**: Store your settings locally for convenience

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data Lives
- **All data is stored locally** in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`
- **No cloud storage**: We do not operate any servers
- **No data transmission**: Data only goes between your browser and GitHub's API
- **Your PAT**: Stored only in your browser, never transmitted to us

### Data Retention
- **Local Data**: Remains until you delete it or uninstall the extension
- **GitHub Files**: Created in your own repository, under your control
- **No backups**: We do not retain copies of your data

## Data Sharing

### We Share Data With:
- **GitHub**: Your configured repository (via their official API)
- **No one else**: We have no servers, no analytics, no third parties

### We NEVER:
- ❌ Sell your data
- ❌ Share with advertisers
- ❌ Use for marketing
- ❌ Transfer to third parties
- ❌ Store your data on our servers

## Your Rights and Controls

### Access Your Data
- View all settings in the extension options page
- Your notes are in your own GitHub repository

### Delete Your Data
- **Settings**: Clear via extension options or uninstall the extension
- **GitHub Notes**: Delete directly from your repository
- **Immediate**: Remove the extension to delete all local data

### Data Portability
- All notes are standard markdown files in your GitHub repository
- Export anytime using Git or GitHub's interface

## Permissions Explained

### Why We Need Each Permission:
- **storage**: Save your settings and PAT locally
- **contextMenus**: Add right-click menu for capturing web selections
- **activeTab**: Access the current tab when you trigger a capture

### Host Permissions:
- **chatgpt.com / chat.openai.com**: Inject capture buttons on ChatGPT
- **api.github.com**: Create files in your repository

## Children's Privacy

chat2repo is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
